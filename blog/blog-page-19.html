Promoting Inclusion and Diversity through the Power of Mobility.


Posted on 13 February 2023 - Author (Jithmi Deshanjalee)

Jithmi Deshanjalee

CQHRM, Charted Institute Of Personal Management(CIPM)
Human Resource Management Intern at ELZIAN AGRO


In Sri Lanka, the agricultural sector is a vital part of the economy, providing employment for a large portion of the population. As in many industries, there is a need to increase diversity and inclusion within the agricultural workforce. One way to do this is by aligning inclusion and diversity with mobility. By providing opportunities for mobility, organizations can create a more inclusive and diverse workplace while also ensuring they have the talent they need to meet their business goals. To align inclusion and diversity with mobility in the agricultural sector in Sri Lanka, organizations can implement a range of strategies. This could involve creating mentorship programs that connect employees from underrepresented groups with senior leaders who can provide guidance and support for their career development.

Additionally, organizations can provide training and development opportunities for employees from diverse backgrounds to ensure that they have the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in mobility assignments. This could include language training, cross-cultural training, and other types of support to help employees thrive in new and unfamiliar environments. By supporting employees in this way, organizations can create a more inclusive and diverse workplace while also building a more skilled and capable workforce.

In conclusion, Elzian Agro is committed to promoting inclusion and diversity through mobility in the agricultural sector in Sri Lanka. By aligning inclusion and diversity with mobility, we are creating a workplace that is more inclusive and diverse while also ensuring that we have the talent we need to meet our business goals. We will continue to implement policies and practices that support both inclusion and mobility to create a workplace where all employees can thrive.

Reference

A description about the topic mobility -- In the context of work, mobility refers to the ability of employees to move or transition within an organization or across different job roles, departments, projects, or geographic locations. It involves the flexibility and adaptability of employees to take on new responsibilities, acquire diverse skills, and work in various settings or locations as needed by the organization. Work mobility can include opportunities for promotion, lateral moves, cross-functional assignments, international assignments, remote work arrangements, and other forms of job rotation or flexibility. It is aimed at enhancing employee development, promoting organizational agility, and meeting the evolving needs of the business.
