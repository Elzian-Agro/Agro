Green Agriculture through a Green Workplace.


Posted on Sunday 05 March 2023 - Author (Eeshani Krishna Madhuwanthi)

Does ' Being Eco-friendly 'work as a help for creating a green world? According to the UK government, a workplace which works under the 'Green concept' will have a great benefit for not just the business but for the whole world. In the modern era, having a 'Green Workplace' is a valuable asset for reaching a good outcome; especially from the agricultural sector. Because modern agriculture faces many challenges. Pollution, biodiversity loss, soil degradation, water scarcity, carbon foot-print & natural resource depletion are some of the major challenges to the sustainability of the world's agriculture. Since sustainable agriculture addresses natural pollution, cares for living beings & saves natural resources; the time has come to support sustainability. So a workplace; which is environmentally sensitive, resource efficient & socially responsible, can be a key for it. Additionally, a green workplace can also improve employee well-being, increase productivity & attract environmentally-conscious consumers. Overall, being eco-friendly and creating green workplaces can play a crucial role in promoting sustainability and protecting our planet's resources for future generations.

As organizations become increasingly aware of the impact of their operations on the environment, the role of HRM in promoting sustainability and environmental responsibility; has become more important. As a vision for creating 'green griculture' ; here are some ways in which the HRM process in a workplace should be organized

1. Set Sustainable Goals

Creating a sustainable and green agriculture system in the workplace can be a challenging task, but it is essential for preserving the environment and ensuring long-term success. A workplace which can promote organic farming practices, biodiversity, renewable energy sources; will be the major guide for employees to support sustainable agriculture. Companies in the modern world like Elzian Agro, which always support sustainable agriculture; are practicing sustainable goals with employees for creating a green agriculture with green workplace. The UK government is the one who started this with workplaces at first.

2. Educated Employees

Educating employees who are well aware about the importance of sustainability and the actions ; they can take to support a green agriculture system can help to create a culture of sustainability in the workplace. This can be achieved through training sessions, workshops, and other educational programs. Elzian Agro is the best example for it. Employees who are open to learning and continuously improving can help in keeping up with the latest sustainable agriculture practices. Do you know?The world has started to run with Agriculture.

3. Promote 'One Family'

Employees who are innovative and creative, can come up with new ideas for sustainable agriculture practices. They can find new ways to reduce the use of pesticides and fertilizers, conserve water, and reduce waste. Good communication & collaboration between all employees is the best path for it. Employees who are good communicators and collaborators can help in building partnerships with other stakeholders such as farmers, suppliers, and customers. They can work together to develop sustainable agriculture practices that benefit veryone. According to a latest report of ILO( The International Labour Organization), workers who are of any educated or social background, can create a green environment, green world. Good communication & collaboration with employees is the main need for it.

Reference

4. Admiration & Rewarding

When employees feel appreciated and recognized for their hard work, they are more likely to feel motivated and committed to their job. This can result in increased productivity, better performance, and a positive attitude towards their work. Supporting green agriculture is an important cause that can benefit not only the environment but also the society as a whole. By encouraging and supporting employees to take part in initiatives that promote green agriculture, companies can contribute to a sustainable future while also enhancing their corporate social responsibility. Many agri companies in the world have started to introduce professional development opportunities to their employees as rewardings. The Department of Agriculture in the US is the best example for it.

5. Interactive Sessions

Interactive sessions in a workplace allow individuals to share their knowledge and ideas on green agriculture. These also provide opportunities for collaboration and networking among individuals with similar interests in green agriculture. Interactive sessions can serve as a platform for education and training on green agriculture practices. And also can be used to identify challenges and problems related to green agriculture, and to develop solutions to challenges. Modern companies in every sectors organize these kind of sessions always. By implementing these green HRM practices, companies like Elzian Agro which works friendly with agriculture can promote sustainability in agriculture and contribute to a more sustainable future.
