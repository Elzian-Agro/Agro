Smart Aquaponics System

The Elzian-Agro-Aqua Automation System is an advanced solution designed to optimize farming and aquaponics by automating key processes such as water quality management, fish sorting, and nutrient regulation. Equipped with smart sensors, the system continuously monitors pH levels, oxygen concentration, and temperature to maintain an optimal environment for both fish and plants.

This automation system reduces manual labor, improves resource efficiency, and enhances productivity, making it ideal for home-based setups and large-scale commercial aquaponics farms. By ensuring a sustainable and balanced ecosystem, it supports healthier fish growth and higher crop yields with minimal intervention.

Water Quality Monitoring

Our system continuously monitors essential water parameters such as pH, temperature, dissolved oxygen, and ammonia levels, ensuring a healthy environment for fish and plants.

About Studio

The Elzian-Agro-Aqua Fish Sorting System is an automated solution designed to efficiently separate fish based on gender while accurately counting them. Using advanced sensors and AI-driven technology, the system identifies male and female fish, ensuring precise sorting without human intervention.
This system is designed to sort popular aquaculture and ornamental fish species, including
Platy, Guppy, Molly, Tetra and Swordtail.
