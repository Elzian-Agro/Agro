ELZIAN AGRO provides smart agronomy solutions to support mass cultivation farmers to monitor and automate their farmland. We offer customized monitoring information and automation solutions through trending digital technologies to optimize our customers' harvest and productively utilize their resources. According to United Nations Sustainable Reports, farmers negatively impact due to extreme weather changes and limited resources. ELZIAN AGRO is a start-up that provides a soil and weather monitoring system for indoor and mass cultivation farmers and supports them to automate their farmland. We offer customized monitoring information and automation solutions through the sensor network to optimize our customers' harvest and productively utilize their resources. Our customers can monitor their farmland anywhere, anytime, and make efficient and rational decisions to maximize their profitability.

Our History

ELZIAN AGRO has initially established after secured a place in the national top teams in the GENERATION UNLIMITED GLOBAL YOUTH CHALLENGE which was organized by the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP), United Nations Children’s Fund (UNICEF), and the Ministry of Education of Sri Lanka. Before being formed as ELZIAN PVT LTD, it was recognized as a partnership (The Agro-Innovatives) among 5 undergraduates from the University of Kelaniya representing the Faculty of Commerce and Management Studies and Faculty of Science. ELZIAN AGRO managed to mark its reputation in several global innovations and local entrepreneurial competitions such as DIGITAL INCLUSION GLOBAL CHALLENGE 2020 organized by Convergence.Tech - Canada, Inter-University Business Plan Competition (BIZ MINDS 2021), and ERICSSON INNOVATION GLOBAL AWARDS 2020.
